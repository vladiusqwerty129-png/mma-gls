Our Story

Built by Community.
For the Community.

Gold Sport Life was founded in 2024 by a group of Ukrainians who wanted to create a place where people could grow physically, mentally, and creatively. What started as a vision became a movement — a club that blends sports, fitness, and the arts into one welcoming family.

We don't just train bodies — we build confidence, friendships, and a sense of belonging. Whether you're 6 or 60, a total beginner or an experienced athlete, there's a place for you here.

🎯 Our Mission

To inspire health, confidence, and creativity through movement and expression — one person, one class, one community at a time.
