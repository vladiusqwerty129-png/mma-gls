Our Story

Built by Fighters.
For the Community.

Gold Sport Life was founded in 2024 by a group of Ukrainians who wanted to create a place where people of all ages could build strength, discipline, and confidence through combat sports.

We're not just a gym — we're a family. Whether you're a 6-year-old stepping on the mat for the first time or an adult looking to get in fighting shape, our doors are open and our coaches are ready.

🎯 Our Mission

To build confident, disciplined, and resilient people — one round at a time. Through MMA and Wrestling, we transform lives for kids and adults alike.
